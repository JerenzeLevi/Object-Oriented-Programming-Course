# 📝 Examination Tasks

## 🚀 1. Speed Level Increase Based on Time

* After every **1 minute** of gameplay, the car speed increases by **+5 units**.
* Difficulty increases are limited to **three times only**.

---

## 🧮 2. Real-Time Score Display

A real-time score system was implemented.

### Scoring Rule:

* 🎯 Player earns **+1 point** for every second survived.

---

## 💥 3. Game Reset After Collision

When collision occurs:

* The game stops immediately.
* A **Game Over** message appears.
* Final score and gameplay duration are displayed.
* A **Restart/Reset** button allows the player to instantly play again without restarting the program.
